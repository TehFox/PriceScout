Privacy Policy

Price Lookup – Find & Compare Any Product  ·  Last updated: March 2026

📋 Summary (Plain English)

Price Lookup does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. It reads product information from the page you are currently viewing — entirely on your device — and uses it to open price comparison searches in new tabs. Nothing about you or your browsing is ever sent to us or any third party.

The sections below explain the technical details.

🔍 What Data the Extension Reads

When you are on a product page, Price Lookup reads the following information directly from that page, locally on your device:

  • Product title — extracted from the page heading or structured data
  • Product price — displayed price on the page (if present)
  • Product image URL — a link to the product photo
  • Page URL and hostname — to identify which store you are on

This data is held only in your browser's session memory (cleared when you close the browser) and is used solely to populate the extension panel and generate search queries on the stores you choose.

Price Lookup never reads any other page content, form fields, passwords, payment information, or personal details.

🌐 Network Requests Made by the Extension

The extension makes one optional outbound request:

Affiliate codes update
URL: https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/TehFox/PriceLookup@main/affiliates.json
When: When the extension panel opens, once per session.
What is sent: Nothing. This is a plain HTTP GET request with no cookies, no user identifiers, and no tracking parameters. It simply downloads the latest list of affiliate codes (e.g. Amazon Associates tag) for use when you click a store button.

This request is made to jsDelivr, a public CDN. jsDelivr may log standard server access data (IP address, timestamp, file requested) subject to their own privacy policy. Price Lookup does not receive or retain any data from this exchange.

All other network requests originate from you — when you click a store button (e.g. Amazon, Wayfair), your browser opens that store's website in a normal new tab. That request is subject to the destination store's own privacy policy.

💛 Referral (Affiliate) Codes

Price Lookup offers an optional, user-controlled referral code feature. Here is exactly how it works:

  • Opt-in only. Referral codes are disabled by default. They are only activated if you explicitly click "Enable referral codes" inside the extension. You can disable them at any time from Settings.
  • Triggered only by your actions. A referral code is appended to a store URL only when you click a store button inside the Price Lookup panel. The extension never modifies URLs you navigate to on your own.
  • No extra cost to you. Referral codes do not change the price you pay. When a purchase is made through a tracked link, the retailer pays a small commission to the developer — at no additional cost to you.
  • Transparent. When enabled, a 💛 indicator appears in the panel header. The affiliate code appended to URLs is visible in the browser address bar.
  • No personal data shared. The only information the affiliate tag carries is a static code identifying Price Lookup as the referrer — never your name, account, or any personal identifier.

Affiliate programmes used include Amazon Associates, Commission Junction, and similar networks. Each network's handling of click data is governed by their own privacy policies.

👶 Children's Privacy

Price Lookup is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The extension does not collect personal data from any user.
